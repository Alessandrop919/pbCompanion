ABOUT PlanetB is a free-roam multiplayer game based on space exploration where players will have the opportunity to cooperate or clash with other players in an immersive, brand new multiplayer experience.

Wandering around galaxies and celestial bodies, you will have a chance peek through the beauty of the infinite, unknown deep space, but keep your eyes open! Dangers hide in every corner.

This game is currently under development and its actual state does not represent how the game will look upon release.
